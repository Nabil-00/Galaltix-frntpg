Export & International Trade Services

Navigating international trade regulations can be complex. Our export division simplifies the entire process, managing documentation, compliance, and logistics to ensure your products reach global markets efficiently and legally.

Key Capabilities

  • Complete Documentation: We handle all export paperwork including phytosanitary certificates, certificates of origin, and customs declarations.
  • Regulatory Compliance: Expert knowledge of international trade regulations, NAFDAC requirements, and destination country standards.
  • Shipping Coordination: Partnerships with reliable freight forwarders for sea, air, and land transportation.
  • Market Access: Established relationships with buyers in 25+ countries across Asia, Europe, and the Middle East.

Our Export Process

We've successfully exported over 15,000 metric tons of agricultural products to international markets, with a 98% on-time delivery rate. Our team stays updated on changing trade policies and maintains strong relationships with customs officials and regulatory bodies.

  • Documentation Preparation: Complete export documentation including certificates, permits, and declarations
  • Quality Pre-Shipment Inspection: Final quality checks before shipment to ensure compliance
  • Customs Clearance: Smooth customs processing at origin and destination
  • Shipping & Logistics: Coordinated transportation with tracking and insurance
  • Delivery Confirmation: Proof of delivery and post-shipment support

  • Europe: Netherlands, Germany, UK, France, Spain, Italy
  • Middle East: UAE, Saudi Arabia, Turkey, Egypt
  • Africa: South Africa, Ghana, Kenya, Morocco

Export Documentation We Handle

International trade requires meticulous documentation. We manage all paperwork to ensure smooth customs clearance and compliance with international regulations.

  • Phytosanitary Certificates: Plant health certificates from Nigerian authorities
  • Certificate of Origin: Proof of product origin for customs and trade agreements
  • Quality Certificates: Laboratory test results and quality attestations
  • Fumigation Certificates: When required for pest-free shipments
  • Bill of Lading: Shipping documents and cargo manifests
  • Commercial Invoice: Detailed transaction documentation
