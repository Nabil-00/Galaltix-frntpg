Who We Are

Driving Progress, Delivering Excellence

Galaltix Nigeria Limited is a diversified export and trading company dedicated to connecting Africa's rich agricultural and natural resources with global markets. We specialize in the sourcing, processing, and exportation of premium agricultural commodities such as sesame seeds, raw cashew nuts, dried hibiscus flowers, cocoa, and non-GMO soybeans.

Since our establishment in 2010, Galaltix has built a strong reputation for reliability, integrity, and quality. Our operations span across major international trade corridors — exporting to leading industrial nations such as Vietnam, India, Turkey, and China. With every shipment, we reaffirm our commitment to delivering excellence and empowering sustainable trade relationships.

At Galaltix, our mission is simple yet profound — to transform opportunities into lasting value for farmers, partners, and clients alike. Headquartered in Nigeria, we combine innovation, professionalism, and sustainability to drive progress in the agro-commodity export sector.

Our Story

GALALTIX JOURNEY

Laying the Foundation

In our formative years, Galaltix established solid partnerships with local farmers, cooperatives, and aggregators across Nigeria's agricultural belt. These relationships form the backbone of our reliable supply chain and quality assurance process.

Expanding Horizons

By 2022, we achieved a major breakthrough — successfully exporting thousands of metric tons of raw cashew nuts and sesame seeds to Asian and Middle Eastern markets. This expansion highlighted our growing influence in international trade and our ability to meet global demand consistently.

Consistent Growth and Performance

Galaltix recorded steady growth year after year, with export volumes and client portfolios increasing exponentially. Our turnover rose from modest beginnings to multi-million-dollar performance, demonstrating our focus on efficiency and customer trust.

Building the Future

With an ambitious outlook for 2025 and beyond, we are projecting sustained growth, targeting over USD 10 million in export value. This milestone underscores our dedication to scaling responsibly while maintaining the high standards that define our brand.

Empowering Local Communities

We believe that true success is shared success. Galaltix invests in capacity-building initiatives for smallholder farmers, promotes fair trade, and supports inclusive growth through local sourcing and employment generation.
