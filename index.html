F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of agricultural products do you supply?

We supply a wide range of agricultural products, including grains (like sorghum, maize, soybeans), tubers (yam, cassava, potatoes), and fresh produce (tomatoes, onions, peppers). Contact us for a detailed list of available products.

Where do you source your products from?

We have a strong network of partner farms across various states in Nigeria. We work directly with local farmers to ensure we source high-quality, fresh, and sustainably grown produce.

Do you offer export services?

Yes, we specialize in export services. We manage the entire process, from quality inspection and custom packaging to documentation and international logistics, ensuring your products reach their destination safely and on time.

What are your quality assurance standards?

Quality is our top priority. All our products undergo rigorous quality control checks and grading processes to meet both Nigerian and international standards. We are committed to delivering only the best to our clients.

Can I place a bulk order?

Absolutely. We are equipped to handle large-volume and bulk orders for both local and international clients. Please contact our sales team through the contact form or email for a custom quote.

What regions do you deliver to?

We have a robust distribution network that covers all major states within Nigeria. We also have the capacity to export to various countries globally. Contact us to discuss your specific delivery needs.
