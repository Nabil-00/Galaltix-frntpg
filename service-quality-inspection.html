Quality Inspection & Grading Services

Quality is non-negotiable in agricultural trade. Our rigorous inspection and grading processes ensure that every product meets specified standards, protecting your reputation and ensuring customer satisfaction.

Key Capabilities

  • Multi-Point Inspection: Products are inspected at sourcing, storage, and pre-shipment stages.
  • Laboratory Testing: Access to certified labs for moisture content, aflatoxin levels, and other critical parameters.
  • International Standards: Grading based on ISO, CODEX, and destination-specific quality requirements.
  • Detailed Reporting: Comprehensive quality reports with photographic documentation for transparency.

Inspection Parameters

Our quality control team includes trained agricultural scientists and certified inspectors with years of experience in commodity grading. We maintain a 98% quality approval rate from international buyers, reflecting our commitment to excellence.

  • Physical Inspection: Visual assessment of color, size, shape, and appearance
  • Moisture Content: Precise moisture level testing using calibrated equipment
  • Purity Analysis: Detection and quantification of foreign matter and impurities
  • Aflatoxin Testing: Laboratory analysis for mycotoxin contamination
  • Pest Infestation: Thorough checks for live or dead insects

Grading Standards

We grade products according to international and destination-specific standards, ensuring your products meet buyer expectations.

  • Grade A/Premium: Highest quality for premium markets
  • Grade B/Standard: Good quality for standard commercial use
  • Grade C/Industrial: Suitable for processing and industrial applications
  • Custom Grading: Tailored grading based on specific buyer requirements

Laboratory Services

We partner with certified laboratories to provide comprehensive testing services that meet international standards.

  • Moisture Analysis: Accurate moisture content determination
  • Mycotoxin Testing: Aflatoxin and other mycotoxin detection
  • Pesticide Residue: Screening for chemical residues
  • Nutritional Analysis: Protein, oil content, and other nutritional parameters
  • Microbiological Testing: Bacterial and fungal contamination checks
