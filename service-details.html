Agricultural Sourcing: Direct from Nigerian Farms

At Galaltix Nigeria Limited, we pride ourselves on our extensive network of trusted farmers and agricultural cooperatives across Nigeria. Our sourcing service connects you directly with high-quality agricultural produce that meets both local and international standards.

  • Direct Farm Partnerships: We work with over 500 verified farms across Nigeria, ensuring consistent supply and quality control from the source.
  • Quality Assurance: Every product is inspected at the farm level to meet export standards before procurement.
  • Sustainable Practices: We promote and support sustainable farming methods that benefit both farmers and the environment.
  • Fair Trade Commitment: Our sourcing ensures fair prices for farmers while maintaining competitive rates for buyers.

Our sourcing team has deep knowledge of Nigeria's agricultural landscape, from the sesame fields of Benue to the ginger farms of Kaduna. We understand seasonal variations, regional specialties, and quality indicators that matter most to international buyers.

Whether you need bulk quantities of staple crops or specialty organic products, our sourcing service provides reliable access to Nigeria's rich agricultural resources. We handle all aspects of procurement, from farmer negotiations to initial quality checks, ensuring you receive products that meet your exact specifications.

Export & International Trade Services

Navigating international trade regulations can be complex. Our export division simplifies the entire process, managing documentation, compliance, and logistics to ensure your products reach global markets efficiently and legally.

  • Complete Documentation: We handle all export paperwork including phytosanitary certificates, certificates of origin, and customs declarations.
  • Regulatory Compliance: Expert knowledge of international trade regulations, NAFDAC requirements, and destination country standards.
  • Shipping Coordination: Partnerships with reliable freight forwarders for sea, air, and land transportation.
  • Market Access: Established relationships with buyers in 25+ countries across Asia, Europe, and the Middle East.

We've successfully exported over 15,000 metric tons of agricultural products to international markets, with a 98% on-time delivery rate. Our team stays updated on changing trade policies and maintains strong relationships with customs officials and regulatory bodies.

Warehousing & Storage Solutions

Proper storage is critical for maintaining product quality. Our modern warehousing facilities are designed to preserve the freshness, nutritional value, and market appeal of agricultural products throughout the supply chain.

  • Climate-Controlled Facilities: Temperature and humidity-controlled environments optimized for different product types.
  • Pest Management: Comprehensive pest control systems that maintain product integrity without chemical contamination.
  • Inventory Management: Real-time tracking systems for complete visibility of your stored products.
  • Flexible Storage Options: Short-term and long-term storage solutions tailored to your business needs.

Our warehouses are strategically located near major transportation hubs, reducing transit times and costs. We employ best practices in warehouse management, including FIFO (First In, First Out) inventory rotation and regular quality monitoring.

Quality Inspection & Grading Services

Quality is non-negotiable in agricultural trade. Our rigorous inspection and grading processes ensure that every product meets specified standards, protecting your reputation and ensuring customer satisfaction.

  • Multi-Point Inspection: Products are inspected at sourcing, storage, and pre-shipment stages.
  • Laboratory Testing: Access to certified labs for moisture content, aflatoxin levels, and other critical parameters.
  • International Standards: Grading based on ISO, CODEX, and destination-specific quality requirements.
  • Detailed Reporting: Comprehensive quality reports with photographic documentation for transparency.

Our quality control team includes trained agricultural scientists and certified inspectors with years of experience in commodity grading. We maintain a 98% quality approval rate from international buyers, reflecting our commitment to excellence.

Supply Chain Consulting & Optimization

Efficient supply chains drive profitability. Our consulting services help businesses optimize their agricultural supply chains for maximum efficiency, cost-effectiveness, and reliability.

  • Process Analysis: Comprehensive review of your current supply chain to identify bottlenecks and inefficiencies.

  • Risk Management: Identification and mitigation of supply chain risks including weather, political, and market factors.

Custom Packaging & Processing Solutions
