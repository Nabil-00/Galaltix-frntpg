Premium Pigeon Peas from Nigeria

Pigeon peas (Cajanus cajan), commonly called "Fio-fio" in Nigeria, are a hardy legume crop valued for their high protein content and versatility. Cultivated extensively across northern Nigeria, the crop thrives in semi-arid conditions with moderate rainfall.

Plants mature within 4–6 months, and pods are harvested once they turn brown and dry. The peas are shelled, cleaned, and dried to safe moisture levels for export. GALALTIX supplies machine-cleaned pigeon peas suited for use in food processing, animal feed, and global pulse trade.

  • Variety: Nigerian White / Brown Type
  • Scientific Name: Cajanus cajan
  • Moisture Content: 12% maximum

Nutritional Benefits

Pigeon peas are a nutritional powerhouse, offering numerous health benefits that make them a valuable addition to any diet.

  • High Protein Content: Excellent source of plant-based protein (20–24%)
  • Rich in Fiber: Promotes digestive health and helps maintain healthy blood sugar levels
  • Essential Minerals: Contains iron, magnesium, phosphorus, and potassium
  • Vitamins: Good source of B-complex vitamins and folate
  • Low Fat: Naturally low in fat and cholesterol-free
